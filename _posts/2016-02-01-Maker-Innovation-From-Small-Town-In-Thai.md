### Maker Innovation from small town in Thailand, ChiangMai Maker Party 2016 report.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*iuGHCYxoVYU6iWUMOcuV6g.jpeg)Hippie westan people make a community in ChiangMai, some people are nomad worker.

ChiangMai well known beautiful and historical city, at north of the Thailand. There is not only ensient city but also creative city by international people. That unique combination between intelligent local people and international creator they still existing happen. I will tell that social environment and result.

Chiang Mai is really a creative city by international people. There have some function such as cheep and safety, small cozy town and exciting creative community, exotic Asian town and intelligent local citizen. Their issues are opposed each other, but Chiang Mai still keeps satisfied any function. You are able to see both of community that the creative international community of nomad workers, engineers and designers, and intelligent Thai peoples community of ChiangMai university.

They are helping each other in same creative ecosystems in town. That is the reason why good cafe, co-working place and maker community in ChiangMai.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*JASGMSYYWlfvwNqo3SC9ow.jpeg)Good design cafe. That sweets is only 2 dollars.![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*4ZZlo524yh3lMypjexpRdw.jpeg)Co-working place at ChiangMai

Nati Sang founder of [Makerspace Thailand,](https://www.facebook.com/makerspaceth) from US, he organized that beautiful co-working place called [punplace](http://www.chiangmaicitylife.com/citylife-articles/makerspace-the-future-will-be-open-source/). He told his works experience and philosophy at ChiangMai Maker Party. [Makerspace Thailand: An innovation lab for everyone]

Makerspace Thailand: An innovation lab for everyone by Nati Sang

ChiangMai Maker Party organized by the ChiangMai Maker Club, start from last year. That is an international event, including Penang, Singapore, US, Scandinavia, Japanese, and some other country.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*C70qJ014FVl2W7eDFWrIZw.jpeg)International Speakers![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*J1Jzki58qF0s7dcFT4gvfA.jpeg)Enjoy people, Kids and Geeks

ChiangMai Maker Club improves ESPRESSO Arduino compatible Wifi IoT toolkit. They often make workshop at all around southeast Asia. I saw Phuket maker group make booth in CMMP2016. And They got experience about own Open Source Hardware toolkit prototyping, produce, promote, that experience effort much higher quality of event than last year.

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*0xHzJQ2OywvT6-Gf7Sy1JA.jpeg)ESPRESSO by [ESpart](http://www.espert.co/).
