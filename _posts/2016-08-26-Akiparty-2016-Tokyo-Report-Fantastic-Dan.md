### AkiParty 2016 Tokyo report-Fantastic Dance Party for Geeks (include 2 performance video)

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/800/1*W_eHj9qy3vUKBTxuChSW9A.jpeg)AkiParty — Dance Party for the Geeks, by the Geeks, of the Geeks (Photo by Akira Kumagai)

[AkiParty — Dance Party for the Geeks, by the Geeks, of the Geeks](https://www.facebook.com/Akiparty4Geeks/).That starts from 2014 in Akihabara, Tokyo, and grown in Shenzhen.  
Then, AkiParty be an official party at Maker Faire Shenzhen 2015.  
Official Facebook Page:[AkiParty — Dance Party for the Geeks](https://www.facebook.com/Akiparty4Geeks/)

Akimake Party Shenzhen MakerFaire 2015 from stage! You can see some famous Makers, such as Eric Pan, Kevin Lau, Meiwa Denki, Massimo Benzi and Tom Igoe. (but this video’s taken by Smartphone, bad quality sound)

At 2016, Akiparty back again to Livehouse MOGRA, Akihabara, Tokyo, Last day of Maker Faire Tokyo. That is not an official event at Tokyo, but Over hundred geeks enjoy LED Cosplay, making trough dancing, some interactive project, anime and 8-bit music.

Ca5 plays 8-bit Chip-tune music by Own music player software by MAX/MSP and some DJ tool. (Photo by Akira Kumagai)

[Ca5 ](http://ca5.me/)plays 8-bit Chip-tune music by Own music player software by MAX/MSP and some DJ tool. Every makers enjoy his music and Maker mind. He did express how to make and play his performance when his time, you can see that video 15:22.

The final act is [Toriena](http://www.toriena.net/). She plays pop-style Chip-Tune by her hacked Game boy and own voice.Her so hot performance made over hundred geeks dance on floor!
